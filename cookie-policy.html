2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to website owners. Cookies can be "persistent" or "session" cookies.

3. How We Use Cookies

  • Performance Cookies: Enhance the performance and functionality of our website

These cookies are necessary for the website to function and cannot be switched off in our systems. They are usually only set in response to actions made by you which amount to a request for services, such as setting your privacy preferences or filling in forms.

4.2 Analytics Cookies

We use Google Analytics to help us understand how our visitors use the website. The information collected is anonymous and statistical in nature. These cookies help us improve our website's performance and user experience.

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can:
